About

THE MIND
BEHIND THE
CREATION

Jose Luis "Pepe" Pagan is a multi-disciplinary creative force whose work spans music production, television, songwriting, film scoring, and intellectual property creation.

As the founder and driving force behind UPM Productions and its family of companies, Pepe brings a rare ability to operate simultaneously at the highest levels of music and entertainment — crafting hit records, developing original TV series, and building intellectual properties from the ground up.

His vision goes beyond individual projects. Pepe architects entire creative ecosystems — connecting music, story, brand, and culture into cohesive entertainment experiences that resonate across markets and platforms.
